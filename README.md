# Enviri (formerly Harsco)

Enviri Corporation, formerly known as Harsco Corporation, is a trusted global leader in environmental solutions, developing and refining methods of recycling and beneficial reuse for industrial customers. The company rebranded from Harsco to Enviri in 2023 to better reflect its focus on environmental services. Enviri operates through three divisions: Clean Earth, Harsco Environmental, and Harsco Rail.

**APIs.json:** [apis.yml](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/api-evangelist/harsco/refs/heads/main/apis.yml)

## APIs

Enviri does not currently publish a public developer portal or open APIs. Integrations with Enviri's environmental services and rail solutions are typically delivered through direct customer engagements with the relevant business unit.
